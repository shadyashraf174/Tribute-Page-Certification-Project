Dr. Magdi Yacoub

The heart surgeon who transformed lives worldwide

Here's a timeline of Dr. Yacoub's life:

  • 1935 - Born in Belbes, Egypt.
  • 1957 - Graduates from Cairo University with a degree in Medicine.
  • 1973 - Becomes a consultant cardiothoracic surgeon at Harefield Hospital.
  • 2003 - Knighted by Queen Elizabeth II for his contributions to medicine.
  • 2008 - Establishes the Magdi Yacoub Heart Foundation.

"My aim is to bring the highest standards of heart care to everyone, especially the underprivileged."

-- Dr. Magdi Yacoub
